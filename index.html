Features

Agent View

With Agent View, HomeLynk provides search analytics to help you and your client make the most of our technology. Access each of your client’s personal profiles, search history, home preferences, and saved homes to ensure you have the knowledge to make the best recommendations in both immediate and future transactions.

BlockBusta

Turn your passive home seeker into an active home buyer with Blockbusta: users save their home preferences and are notified by text as soon as a home that matches their criteria hits the market. With automated updates, Blockbusta keeps your clients up-to-date 24/7, ensuring that you and your client are the first to act and close the deal in red-hot real estate markets – and you don’t have to lift a finger.

Real-time Search

Leveraging your unique access as an MLS partner, HomeLynk gives your clients an edge in the home buying process with access to the most updated, comprehensive, and accurate data in the industry. No more stale listings, misinformation, or wasted time meaning more potential sales.

Direct Agent Contact

Whether asking a question about a home, requesting more information, or scheduling a tour, your client can contact you about a specific listing with the tap of a finger – all within the confines of the app. This way, clients can swiftly and instantly connect with the most trusted provider of real estate information: you.

About Us
